Secret of Fantasy 2 - The Epic Battle
============================

By Team Darkbits

Rules:
====

* Deities & Demigods
	On of the characters is a god. However due to lack of time you will never find that out in the compo version of the game.
* Hand drawn
	Backgrounds and enemies have a computer hand drawn look a la Yoshi's Island.
* Use samples of human(-like) voice
	Not implemented.
* Polymorphism
	Everything in the game use polymorphism.
* View source
	Robo in the game has a hack ability that displays source.
